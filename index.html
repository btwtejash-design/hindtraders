Our Profile

Built on Reliability & Compliance

Hind Traders, established in 2007 by Shri Satish Kumar, is a proprietary firm dedicated to strengthening the supply chain of the Eastern Railway. Based in the historic railway hub of Jamalpur, we bridge the gap between quality manufacturing and government procurement needs.

We pride ourselves on financial discipline, strict adherence to RDSO specifications, and a zero-tolerance policy towards non-compliance.

Our Legacy & Heritage

The Historic Jamalpur Workshop

Hind Traders is proud to operate from Jamalpur, home to the Jamalpur Locomotive Workshop. Established on 8th February 1862, it was the first full-fledged railway workshop facility in India.

ER Official Site Workshop History

Serving Eastern Railway

The Eastern Railway (ER), formed on April 14, 1952, is one of the pillars of Indian transport. We are honored to contribute to this zone, which serves the rich industrial and cultural belts of West Bengal, Bihar, and Jharkhand.

Is Hind Traders a registered Railway Contractor?

We are based in Jamalpur, Munger, Bihar, a strategic hub for the Eastern Railway, enabling us to provide efficient supply chain solutions.

What materials do you supply?

We supply a wide range of railway-approved materials, including mechanical components, hardware, and consumables as per RDSO specifications.
